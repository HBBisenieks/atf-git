Ingredients:
3 Tablespoons butter
3 Tablespoons flour
1 1/2 Cup milk
1 teaspoon ground mustard seed
1 teaspoon paprika
2 Cups (or more) grated cheese
Salt and pepper to taste

1 box rotini or radiatori pasta

In a double-boiler, melt butter on a medium heat. Stir in flour and spices. Slowly fold in milk until mixture is creamy (if the sauce is not thickening, increase heat slightly). Add grated cheese and stir until melted.

Pour sauce over cooked pasta and stir.
